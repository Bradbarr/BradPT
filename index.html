My approach

Holistic coaching that blends training, movement quality, recovery, and lifestyle—so results actually stick.

What you can expect

  • Assessment-led training: goals, movement, constraints (time, equipment, injuries).
  • Clear progression: you’ll know what we’re doing, why, and what comes next.
  • Coaching, not punishing: intensity with purpose—always safe and intelligent.
  • Equipment included: I bring what’s needed so you can focus on showing up.

Training pillars

  • Strength: strong bodies perform better and stay leaner.
  • Conditioning: energy, work capacity, confidence.
  • Mobility & control: move well to train consistently.
  • Nutrition structure: simple habits that suit your routine.
  • Mindset: consistency beats intensity—every time.

FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions—especially around evenings and availability.

Do you only train in the evenings?

Yes—mostly evenings. Sessions are typically around 90 minutes, and I keep slots limited so each client gets proper attention, consistency, and progression.

Do I need equipment?

No. I bring what’s needed for your session. If you have anything at home (bands, dumbbells, bench), I’ll incorporate it.

Where do you cover?

I’m based in Llandeilo and cover Carmarthenshire. If you’re outside the county, message me and I’ll let you know if it’s workable (travel fee may apply).

How many sessions per week is best?

Most clients do 1–3/week. If time is tight, 1/week plus a simple home plan can still work—consistency matters most.
